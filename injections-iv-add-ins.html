Allergy Shots

Allergy shots help train the immune system to respond less aggressively to allergens such as pollen, dust, and pet dander. They are commonly used for long-term relief of seasonal or environmental allergies. Ideal for individuals who experience chronic allergy symptoms that interfere with daily life or sleep quality.

  • •Seasonal allergy relief
  • •Sinus inflammation
  • •Immune support

Alpha Lipoic Acid

Alpha Lipoic Acid (ALA) is a potent antioxidant that protects cells from oxidative stress and helps regenerate other antioxidants like vitamins C and E. It supports nerve health, improves insulin sensitivity, and is often used for anti-aging purposes. This is a great option for those managing neuropathy, diabetes, or chronic fatigue.

  • •Blood sugar regulation
  • •Nerve pain and inflammation
  • •Anti-aging

Amino Acid Blend

This blend of essential amino acids supports muscle repair, improves endurance, and enhances fat metabolism. It’s popular among athletes, those recovering from surgery, or anyone looking to improve overall physical performance. Regular supplementation may also improve focus and reduce muscle fatigue.

  • •Muscle growth
  • •Post-workout recovery
  • •Fat metabolism

Anti-nausea / Zofran

Zofran is a prescription anti-nausea medication commonly used in hospitals and IV therapy for fast relief from nausea and vomiting. It’s ideal for clients recovering from illness, hangovers, migraines, or post-travel fatigue. Zofran works by blocking chemicals in the brain that trigger nausea, offering rapid relief.

  • •Motion sickness
  • •Hangover recovery
  • •Post-IV nausea prevention

Arginine

Arginine is a naturally occurring amino acid that boosts nitric oxide production, which helps improve blood flow and circulation. Often used for cardiovascular health, it also supports muscle recovery and enhances libido in both men and women. Recommended for individuals experiencing fatigue, poor circulation, or low libido.

  • •Improved circulation
  • •Heart health
  • •Libido support

Benadryl

Benadryl (Diphenhydramine) is an antihistamine used to relieve allergy symptoms such as itching, sneezing, and runny nose. It is also effective in treating motion sickness, nausea, and insomnia. When administered through an IV, Benadryl provides rapid relief for acute allergic reactions, such as hives, rashes, or swelling, and can be used as a sedative for those needing relaxation or sleep.

  • •Relieves allergy symptoms (itching, sneezing, congestion)
  • •Reduces nausea and motion sickness
  • •Promotes relaxation and sleep
  • •Quick relief for allergic reactions and rashes

Biotin

Biotin is a B vitamin that plays a key role in the health of your hair, skin, and nails. It supports the body’s keratin infrastructure and may help reduce hair thinning and brittle nails. Commonly chosen by clients looking for beauty support or recovering from postpartum or stress-related hair loss.

  • •Hair growth
  • •Skin health
  • •Nail strength

Dexamethasone

Dexamethasone is a corticosteroid medication that helps reduce inflammation and treat a variety of conditions, including allergies, arthritis, skin disorders, and autoimmune diseases. It is effective in managing severe inflammation, reducing swelling, and controlling pain. This IV is often used in combination with other treatments for faster recovery or in cases of severe flare-ups or allergic reactions.

  • •Reduces inflammation
  • •Relieves pain and swelling
  • •Manages allergic reactions
  • •Used for autoimmune conditions and severe flare-ups

Glutathione

Glutathione is a powerful antioxidant naturally produced in the body that supports detoxification, cellular repair, and skin clarity. It's often used for liver support, immune strengthening, and overall wellness. Clients looking to improve skin tone, fight oxidative stress, or recover from illness frequently request this injection.

  • •Liver health
  • •Immune support
  • •Skin clarity

Hydroxocobalamin B12

Hydroxocobalamin B12 is a slow-release form of B12 that stays active in the system longer than other types. It supports energy, red blood cell production, and neurological health. Often recommended for those with chronic fatigue, vegan diets, or absorption issues, it's also helpful in managing B12 deficiency symptoms.

  • •Chronic fatigue
  • •Neuropathy
  • •Mental clarity

Kenalog

Kenalog is a corticosteroid injection used to reduce inflammation and manage a variety of conditions such as allergies, arthritis, and skin disorders. It is particularly effective in reducing swelling, redness, and pain. This IV is typically used for those with chronic inflammatory conditions, acute flare-ups, or as part of a broader treatment plan to manage autoimmune or allergic reactions.

  • •Inflammation reduction
  • •Pain and swelling relief
  • •Allergic reaction management
  • •Chronic condition flare-up control

L-Carnitine

L-Carnitine is an amino acid that helps transport fatty acids into cells to be used for energy. It supports metabolism, endurance, and exercise recovery, making it a favorite among those with active lifestyles or weight loss goals. It may also benefit heart and brain function.

  • •Fat metabolism
  • •Energy boost
  • •Exercise recovery

L-Taurine

L-Taurine is an amino acid that plays a key role in cardiovascular function, hydration, and nervous system regulation. It’s especially helpful for clients experiencing fatigue, dehydration, or stress-related tension. Often added to IVs for athletes or those seeking enhanced focus and stamina.

  • •Cardiovascular support
  • •Hydration
  • •Mental performance

Lipo-C

Lipo-C (Lipoic Acid + Vitamin C) is a powerful fat-burning and antioxidant infusion that helps improve metabolism, detoxify the body, and promote fat loss. The combination of Lipoic Acid and Vitamin C boosts energy, supports immune function, and assists with healthy weight management. It’s ideal for individuals looking to accelerate their fat loss, improve skin elasticity, or recover from a sluggish metabolism.

  • •Fat metabolism support
  • •Antioxidant protection
  • •Boosts energy and immunity
  • •Supports healthy weight loss

Methylcobalamin B12

Methylcobalamin B12 is the most bioavailable form of B12, ideal for supporting nerve function and brain health. It’s quickly absorbed and often used to combat fatigue, brain fog, and memory issues. This is a preferred choice for individuals with neurological concerns or those needing fast B12 replenishment.

  • •Brain fog
  • •Energy boost
  • •Neurological health

Mineral Blend

The mineral blend is designed to restore key electrolytes and minerals lost through sweat, dehydration, or illness. It supports muscle function, hydration, and nutrient balance. Ideal for athletes, those recovering from illness, or clients feeling depleted from heat exposure or travel.

  • •Electrolyte balance
  • •Muscle cramps
  • •Post-workout recovery

NAD+

NAD+ (Nicotinamide Adenine Dinucleotide) is a coenzyme critical for cellular energy and repair. It helps improve mental clarity, stamina, and mitochondrial function. Frequently requested for anti-aging, burnout recovery, or cognitive performance, NAD+ is popular among high-achieving professionals and wellness-focused individuals.

  • •Fatigue
  • •Brain health
  • •Anti-aging

Pepcid

Pepcid (famotidine) is a histamine-2 blocker that helps reduce stomach acid production. It is often used to relieve symptoms of acid reflux, heartburn, and indigestion. This is a helpful add-on for clients prone to GI upset, especially after certain medications or IV treatments.

  • •Heartburn
  • •Upset stomach
  • •Acid reflux

Skinny Shot

The Skinny Shot is a lipotropic injection designed to support fat loss and energy. It typically contains a blend of B vitamins and amino acids that help metabolize fat and reduce appetite. Great for clients with weight loss goals, sluggish metabolism, or energy dips throughout the day.

  • •Weight management
  • •Fat metabolism
  • •Energy boost

Toradol

Toradol (ketorolac) is a strong, non-steroidal anti-inflammatory medication (NSAID) used for short-term pain relief. It helps reduce inflammation, swelling, and discomfort quickly. Ideal for clients experiencing migraines, back pain, muscle soreness, or joint aches.

  • •Muscle pain
  • •Joint aches
  • •Injury recovery

Tri-Immune: Vitamin C, Zinc, Glutathione

Tri-Immune is a powerhouse blend of Vitamin C, Zinc, and Glutathione formulated to strengthen immune function. It supports antioxidant defense, wound healing, and cellular protection. This blend is popular during cold and flu season, post-travel, or anytime your immune system needs a boost.

  • •Immune support
  • •Illness prevention
  • •Post-illness recovery

Vitamin B Complex (B1, B2, B3, B5, B6)

Vitamin B Complex includes B1, B2, B3, B5, and B6—each vital for energy production, nervous system function, and metabolic efficiency. This blend helps fight fatigue, regulate mood, and improve hair and skin health. It’s a popular option for those under stress, with nutrient deficiencies, or seeking an energy boost without caffeine.

  • •Fatigue
  • •Nervous system support
  • •Metabolic health

Vitamin B12

Vitamin B12 is essential for neurological function, red blood cell formation, and DNA synthesis. It's especially beneficial for individuals with fatigue, vegan or vegetarian diets, or difficulty absorbing nutrients. Injections provide a fast, effective way to restore levels and support mental clarity and energy.

  • •Fatigue
  • •Brain fog
  • •Low B12 levels

Vitamin B6

Vitamin B6 supports brain development and immune function while also playing a role in mood regulation and hormone balance. It is often recommended for individuals experiencing PMS symptoms, low energy, or frequent illness. Regular supplementation may also aid in reducing inflammation and improving sleep quality.

  • •Hormonal balance
  • •Nausea relief
  • •Immune health

Vitamin D

Vitamin D plays a critical role in bone strength, immune regulation, and mood balance. Many adults are deficient without realizing it, especially those who spend limited time in the sun. This injection is perfect for anyone with low energy, weakened immunity, or signs of seasonal affective disorder.

  • •Vitamin D deficiency
  • •Immune health
  • •Mood support

Zinc

Zinc is a trace mineral essential for immune defense, wound healing, and cellular function. It helps shorten the duration of colds, fights inflammation, and supports healthy skin. Ideal for clients during flu season or those recovering from illness, skin issues, or frequent infections.

  • •Cold and flu prevention
  • •Skin healing
  • •Immune boost
