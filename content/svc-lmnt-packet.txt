How LMNT Electrolyte Packets Work in Your Body
Hydration is about more than water—your body also needs electrolytes to function properly. LMNT Electrolyte Packets deliver three key minerals: **sodium, potassium, and magnesium** in ratios that actually meet physiological demands. These nutrients help maintain fluid balance inside and outside your cells, regulate muscle contractions, and keep your nervous system firing efficiently.
During physical activity, fasting, or hot weather, your body loses sodium through sweat. If not replenished, dehydration symptoms can occur, such as headaches, fatigue, muscle cramps, or brain fog. LMNT solves this by offering a high-sodium formula that matches real-world losses, without adding sugar that could spike blood glucose or cause crashes later.
The product is incredibly easy to use. Just mix one packet into 16 to 32 ounces of water and shake or stir. You can scale the concentration to your preference. Fruity flavors like **Citrus**, **Raspberry**, and **Watermelon** are ideal for refreshing cold hydration, while the **Chocolate** line (including **Chocolate Mint**, **Chocolate Raspberry**, **Chocolate Chili**, and **Chocolate Chai**) can also be mixed into hot water for a cozy, nutrient-rich alternative to traditional cocoa drinks.
LMNT fits into keto, paleo, and low-carb protocols and is gluten-free, vegan, and free of artificial additives. It’s trusted by health-conscious individuals, trainers, and elite athletes for a reason—it works without compromise.
