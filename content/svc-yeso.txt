Yeso-Therapy Body Wraps
Sculpt and Tighten with Yeso-Therapy Body Wraps
Yeso-Therapy Body Wraps are a non-invasive contouring method that combines plaster bandages with active ingredients to firm, slim, and detox targeted areas of the body. This therapy involves applying a mineral-rich plaster wrap over the abdomen, thighs, arms, or back. As the wrap hardens, it compresses and stimulates circulation, creating a sculpting effect.
The ingredients used in Yeso wraps help reduce water retention, support lymphatic drainage, and improve skin tightness. It’s a relaxing yet effective treatment, often chosen by clients looking to enhance definition or reduce bloating before an event. Sessions last about 45 to 60 minutes. The wrap remains in place while clients rest, allowing the formula to work while gently reshaping the body. Common results include a smoother appearance, visible inch loss, and firmer skin. A series of treatments provides longer-lasting effects.
Yeso-Therapy is safe, natural, and requires no downtime. It’s suitable for all body types and fits well into a wellness or weight loss regimen.
How Yeso-Therapy Body Wraps Work
Yeso-Therapy works by combining compression from hardened plaster with the action of detoxifying and firming ingredients. The bandages are soaked in a blend of minerals, clays, and natural actives that absorb into the skin during the session. Once applied, the wrap gently compresses the area, helping to flush out excess fluids, support lymph flow, and tone the skin. As the plaster dries, it creates pressure that can result in temporary inch loss and visible contouring. The warmth generated by the wrap also promotes circulation, which aids in detoxification and muscle relaxation. The body responds by tightening the skin and smoothing surface irregularities like mild cellulite. Clients often report feeling lighter, less bloated, and more sculpted after the first session. Continued treatments improve elasticity and help reinforce shape. Because the method is topical and manual, there are no risks associated with surgery or downtime. Yeso-Therapy is ideal for those looking to naturally firm, tone, and define their shape without invasive procedures.
