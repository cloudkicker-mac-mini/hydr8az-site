Balance and Soothe with CBD+ Professional Skincare Line
The CBD+ Professional Skincare Line by Alma blends cannabidiol with proven clinical ingredients to calm, hydrate, and rejuvenate skin. Designed for daily use, this medical-grade line targets common skin concerns like redness, inflammation, dullness, and dehydration. Each formula features high-quality CBD, known for its anti-inflammatory and antioxidant effects. This helps reduce irritation while protecting the skin barrier.
The collection includes targeted kits for anti-aging, pigment correction, post-procedure recovery, and body firming. Popular items include exfoliating pads, brightening serums, balancing cleansers, and firming creams. These products are designed for all skin types, especially sensitive or stressed skin. CBD helps regulate oil production, reduce visible redness, and support a more even tone. Regular use helps restore the skin’s natural equilibrium, improving hydration and texture over time. Unlike typical cosmetic products, CBD+ is developed with dermatological precision, offering visible results for clients who expect high-performance care.
Whether used alone or alongside in-office treatments, the CBD+ line supports healthy, balanced skin every day.
How the CBD+ Professional Skincare Line Works
CBD+ skincare uses cannabidiol (CBD), a compound from hemp, to calm inflammation and improve the skin’s overall function. It interacts with receptors in the skin’s endocannabinoid system, which helps control oil production, irritation, and stress responses. In combination with other active ingredients like peptides, niacinamide, and botanical extracts, each formula supports skin health at the cellular level. For example, the Barrier Balancing Hydrator helps rebuild the skin’s moisture barrier, while the PM Repairing Pads work overnight to support skin renewal. The kits offer curated routines that address specific needs, from post-treatment recovery to brightening and firming. Each product is designed for deep absorption, delivering benefits without clogging pores or causing sensitivity. CBD’s antioxidant action also protects against daily environmental stress. This multi-action system makes the line suitable for anyone with sensitive skin, visible redness, uneven tone, or age-related concerns. The clean formulation avoids harsh additives, making it ideal for patients looking for results without irritation. With consistent use, clients notice improved radiance, better hydration, and a smoother, more balanced complexion.
