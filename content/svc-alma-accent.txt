Alma Accent Prime Body Contouring &amp; Skin Tightening
Sculpt and Tighten with Alma Accent Prime Body Contouring
Alma Accent Prime Body Contouring &amp; Skin Tightening is a non-invasive treatment that merges ultrasound and radiofrequency to reshape the body and firm the skin. Unlike surgical procedures, this technology targets fat deposits and stimulates collagen without downtime. The system delivers focused energy deep into the tissue, breaking down fat cells and boosting skin elasticity. Patients experience visible toning and smoother contours after a series of treatments.
Whether targeting the abdomen, thighs, flanks, or arms, Accent Prime offers an option to refine body shape without surgery. The process improves lymphatic drainage and blood circulation, which aids natural detoxification and supports overall skin health. This treatment stands out for its ability to provide both fat reduction and skin tightening in one session. The dual technology ensures efficient heating and mechanical vibration, maximizing results while minimizing discomfort. Accent Prime suits individuals with mild to moderate skin laxity or localized fat.
Our treatments last between 1 hour and 1 hour 30 minutes. With regular sessions, clients often report firmer skin, reduced circumference, and improved texture, contributing to a more sculpted and youthful appearance.
How Alma Accent Prime Body Contouring Works
Accent Prime combines patented ultrasound shear wave and radiofrequency technologies to achieve body sculpting and skin rejuvenation. Ultrasound waves first target fat cells through mechanical disruption. These waves vibrate and damage the structure of the fat, allowing the lymphatic system to naturally eliminate the debris. Then, radiofrequency energy penetrates the skin to heat the tissue layers, encouraging collagen remodeling and tightening. The combination enables deep treatment with minimal surface impact, making the procedure safe for all skin types. The applicators are designed to be customizable, allowing providers to adjust energy levels and depth based on the patient’s goals. Most patients feel a warm sensation during treatment, with no need for anesthesia. Unlike surgical liposuction, there are no incisions, no scarring, and no significant recovery time. Results develop gradually, improving with each session. Most patients undergo a series of 4–6 treatments spaced weekly. Improvements can be seen in areas like the abdomen, flanks, thighs, upper arms, and neck. This dual-action approach allows Accent Prime to address both subcutaneous fat and loose skin, making it a comprehensive solution for body sculpting and firming.
