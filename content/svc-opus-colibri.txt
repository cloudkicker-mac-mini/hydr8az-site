Opus Colibri Eye Lift &amp; Lesion Removal
Lift and Refine with Opus Colibri Eye Lift &amp; Lesion Removal
Opus Colibri Eye Lift &amp; Lesion Removal offers non-invasive rejuvenation using advanced microplasma technology. Designed for delicate facial areas, it targets sagging skin, fine lines, and skin imperfections without surgery. The device delivers controlled energy through a focused handpiece to stimulate collagen and gently resurface the skin.
Common concerns treated include crow’s feet, upper eyelid laxity, xanthelasma, and perioral wrinkles. Unlike blepharoplasty or ablative lasers, Opus Colibri offers precision with minimal thermal injury and faster recovery. Patients experience firmer skin, fewer wrinkles, and improved skin tone around the eyes and mouth. It’s ideal for clients who want to reduce early signs of aging or address localized lesions without downtime or scarring. Results appear gradually over a few weeks and improve with each session. Safe for all skin types, this FDA-cleared treatment can be performed in under 30 minutes and does not require anesthesia.
For those seeking a gentle yet effective solution to lift and refresh the eye area, Opus Colibri is a standout choice.
How Opus Colibri Eye Lift &amp; Lesion Removal Works
Opus Colibri uses fractional microplasma to stimulate skin remodeling with pinpoint accuracy. It delivers focused thermal energy that contracts skin tissue and encourages collagen production. The treatment causes mild ablation in targeted areas, smoothing wrinkles and tightening skin with low risk of damage to nearby tissue. It’s especially useful for upper and lower eyelids, where precision is critical. Because the device creates tiny channels in the skin, healing is fast and side effects are minimal. In most cases, patients resume normal activities within a day or two. Each session lasts around 15–20 minutes and is well tolerated with topical numbing. Results improve over several weeks as new collagen forms. The treatment also addresses small benign lesions, such as xanthelasma or raised bumps, improving skin clarity and smoothness without surgical excision. This makes Opus Colibri a highly effective option for natural-looking skin renewal in sensitive areas.
